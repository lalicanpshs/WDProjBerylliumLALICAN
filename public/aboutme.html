About Me

Heya guys, I'm Sol. I created this site (with some help from my previous partner), failed_comedian, and the Roulette SMP.

Random Info You Probably Wouldn't Wanna Read But I Put It Anyway

Did you know that I didn't intend to do this project myself?

Originally, the website was supposed to be my partner's OC index. I was supposed to do most of the code, and she was supposed to do most of the content.

And then I got unlucky.

I was then forced to use my own OCs, which I kinda gave up and barely worked on. A while later, I've been working on an SMP. Unfortunately for me, I had the ingenious idea to use that for my website a week before the deadline. But it's a win-win! I end up enjoying working on my website, and I get to advertise the SMP!

I mean, I finished it anyway. Yay.
